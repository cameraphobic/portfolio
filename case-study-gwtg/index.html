Go With The Grain

UX Design + Research Apprenticeship capstone project
School of Visual Concepts · February 2022

Background

Go With The Grain (GWTG) is a fictional online store for everything but the camera: analog film, chemistry, and darkroom equipment. [As an apprentice... I completed SVC while working part time with a design team at Amazon.] I selected this product line for my ecommerce concept project because my professional background is in photography. It was my first job, and I still shoot film for fun. Turns out I’m not alone!
