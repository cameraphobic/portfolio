About Me

UX Designer, Visual Thinker

My heros have always been artists, so imagine my surprise finding a meaningful fit for my skills here in tech. Years spent taking pictures developed my eye for composition, and framing pictures got me hooked on building (analog) products to delight people.

In Spring 2020, I completed General Assembly’s UX Design Immersive course, then returned to assist with teaching as a Designer in Residence. Working alongside design professionals from across the industry has enriched my own workflow, and my passion for digital products.
