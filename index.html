About Me

Hello, I'm Brittany! Product Designer at AWS and cofounder of Slow•Tech Studio

Currently — I'm working on accessible, inclusive features to enhance remote collaboration across Amazon Chime web, desktop, and mobile meeting platforms. At AWS I've fully absorbed the builder mindset and learned to treat every solution like a work-in-progress, where measurable impact matters more than good intentions.

Day-to-day I run projects alongside engineers and partner with PM to boldly explore new opportunity space. I click best with design teams that fall in love with real problems, speak plainly, think critically, and invest in each other’s success.

Formerly — I’ve worn lots of hats in the creative skill trades. Some of my favorites were photographing museum artifacts, framing artwork, and digitally restoring irreplaceable vintage photos. My art background keeps me honest and pushes me to imagine new forms of technology to support human flourishing at scale.

Hobbies include reading, yoga, teaching myself to program, and taking pictures around the Pacific Northwest.
